⚠️ Causes of Breast Cancer

Understanding the risk factors and causes of breast cancer.

  • 🧬 Genetic mutations (BRCA1 & BRCA2 genes).
  • 👵 Aging - risk increases with age.
  • 🩸 Family history of breast cancer.
  • 🍷 Alcohol consumption and smoking.
  • 🏋️‍♀️ Lack of physical activity and obesity.
  • ⚠️ Hormone replacement therapy (HRT).
