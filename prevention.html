🛡 Prevention of Breast Cancer

Steps to reduce the risk of breast cancer through a healthy lifestyle and early detection.

  • 🥗 Maintain a healthy diet with fruits and vegetables.
  • 🏃 Engage in regular physical activity.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • 🔬 Get regular screenings and mammograms.
  • 🩺 Know your family history and discuss risk factors with your doctor.
